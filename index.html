The First Macbook Pro The first Macbook Pro was announced in May of 2006. It cost $1,099 and some key features were the glossy widescreen display and new design. 2006
The First Macbook Air The first Macbook Air was announced in January of 2008. It cost $1,799 and some key features were the measurements. It was the thinnest laptop at the time. 2008
Macbook Air 11 inch The 11 inch Macbook Air was announced in October of 2008 and cost $1,299. It became very popular because of the multi-touch trackpad. 2010
Macbook Pro The new Macbook Pro was announced in June of 2012 and cost $2,199. Some key features were the retina display, lighter design and improved speakers. 2012
Macbook Air The new Macbook Air was announced in November of 2018 and cost $1,099. Some key features were retina display, security chips and the touch ID. 2018
